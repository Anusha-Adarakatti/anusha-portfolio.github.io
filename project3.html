LLM-JND Comparison Research

This innovative research project pioneers the use of Large Language Models (LLMs) to quantify human visual perception through systematic paired comparison experiments. By developing specialized prompt engineering techniques, we enable LLMs to assess Just Noticeable Differences (JND) in image sharpness with remarkable accuracy.
